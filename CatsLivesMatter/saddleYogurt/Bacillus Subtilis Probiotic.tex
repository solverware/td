\section{Bacillus Subtilis}
\textit{Bacillus subtilis} is a probiotic, i.e., when taken in sufficient amounts, it provides a proven health benefit such as supporting gut balance. Certain strains of B. subtilis produce nattokinase and some are even named Bacillus subtilis \textit{natto}.

The \textit{Bacillus subtilis} HU58\^TM strain chosen here is provided by Microbiome Labs. It produces more than two dozen metabolites, among which is nattokinase and short-chain fatty acids (SCFAs). SCFAs are metabolites that typically strengthen the gut barrier, support immunity as well as ward off pathogens.
