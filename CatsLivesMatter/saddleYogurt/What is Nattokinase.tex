\section{What is Nattokinase}
Nattokinase is an enzyme. Enzymes are biological catalysts. Catalysts increase reaction \textit{rates}. An enzyme increases the rate by lowering the amount of energy needed to get a biochemical reaction to occur. A process such as breaking down fibrin clots is not natively rapid. For nattokinase breaking down fibrin clots, picture it as a hydraulic jack under a car: it jacks up and cleaves the structure with minimal effort. For the body to naturally break down a fibrin clot can take months. In a saddle thrombus patient, the rate of clot dissolution should be far greater than the rate of clot formation.
